Our Facilities

# Most Common Features are as Follows:

1. Automated Access control system

Automatic Hardware gate systems like Arm Barriers, Bollards, Blockers can be installed at the entrances of every parking slots as an access management system to parking areas, these gates and blockers can be integrated with any smart identification technologies like RFID Smart Technology, Long Range Tag reading UHF Technology, Ticketing Dispensers, Number Plate Recognitions (ANPR) AND OTHER Sensors. The parking management system is integrated with Access control systems like Automatic gates, barrier controls, ticketing systems etc. These are some of the solutions which can create in helping a fully automated carpark solution.


2. Statistical Reporting software

The software regulates & monitors the parking facility requirements, designed to manage the car parking slots & provide useful reports/information to the management.


3. Parking Guidance Systems (PGS)

A system created to guide drivers to the closest available parking space. Real time information is used to improve and increase the effectiveness of the system.


4. Parking reserving barriers

Type of barriers installed with the purpose of restricting and regulating the access of vehicles and access control devices that help in reserving parking slots.


5. Automated Ticketing systems

The Ticketing System will be programed to the clients parking policies and integrated with the ticketing dispenser machines and Payment systems.



6. Video Surveillance system

CCTV can be integrated with parking management system in creating a secured parking space to help create a safer environment.


6. Reserved Parking for VIP person

A Seperate Served Parking has been given for the VIP person where they can park the vehicle and We will make sure to maintain high secuity for your vehicle service 24x7. Here we Allocate slots as per two-wheeler and four-wheeler vehicles to utilise all available space.


7. Less Parking charges for Women.

For women we take less charges as compared to other people, because we believe that women in the society should be motivated and uplifted and can get equal status as men do.


8. Special Parking Area for Handicap people

We have a Special Parking Area for Handicap people in which we have some staff members which will take a special care to those handicap people so that no problem could be faced by them.
